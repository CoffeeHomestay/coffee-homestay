The first homestay in this district offers a unique experience by adopting the traditional architecture of the Sasak tribe, complete with Western-style bathroom facilities. Located in the heart of a traditional and friendly community, this homestay provides a peaceful atmosphere, far from the hustle and bustle of the city, in a still pristine environment surrounded by lush coffee plantations. Guests can truly experience the calming beauty of nature while enjoying the fresh mountain air.

The local people are incredibly welcoming, offering guests the opportunity to immerse themselves in the daily life of the Sasak tribe. Besides staying and learning alongside the locals, guests can also partake in various other exciting tourism activities, such as a tour around the picturesque Tetebatu village. Here, guests can explore the surrounding natural beauty, sample traditional cuisine, and delve deeper into the local culture and customs.

This homestay is the perfect choice for travelers seeking tranquility, an authentic cultural experience, and the chance to engage directly with the local community in a truly natural and peaceful environment. Other activities that guests can enjoy include visiting waterfalls, rice terraces, and even adventuring at the foot of Mount Rinjani, all of which offer a different sensation from typical vacations.
